Math Jeopardy

About the Event



An outreach program conducted on the national mathematics day, on 22nd December,2021. Math Jeopardy was a math based online contest which questions the participant’s logic. The main objective behind conducting the contest was to encourage School students to make the best use of their math and problem-solving skills and to remember contributions of great Mathematician Srinivasa Ramanujan.

Total of 15 teams(teamsize:2-3) registered and students participated actively in the contest. The students from class 11 of Zion International Public school, Raam Lakshith, M Naveen and Revanth Ashok bagged the first place followed by two teams from Vaels international school from class 10, Hayagreeva Narayanan, Rakshan Sridhar and Vijay Vignesh and from class 12, Vamsidhar Krishnan, Vishal Sairam and Karthik Prakash. And one team SRM Public School from class 11, Kavya came as the runner ups of this contest.
