DUI

Design Ur Interface - UI designing contest

About the Event



DUI was the designing contest conducted by the Design and Innovation Club. The participants with team of two were given 3 topics out of which they have to design a user interface on any one of them. The participants were given one week of time from 25th July 2021 to 31st July 2021 to submit their designs online. The winners got selected on the basis of their creative designs and their extraordinary efforts. Winners got awarded with an e-certificate.


Event: Design Ur Interface
Date of event : 25th July 2021 to 31st July 2021
