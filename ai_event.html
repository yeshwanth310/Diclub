Artificial Intelligence Workshop

About the Event



Artificial Intelligence Workshop was conducted by the Design and Innovation Club of SRM, Vadapalani Campus On the Zoom platform. The first session was held on Jan 27th with around 160+ participants. The Guest Speaker of the session, MR.Jothi Periasamy gave us an insight of Machine learning and how to develop applications from a industrial perspective. He gave an in-depth module planned explanation for the types of applications we use and what kind we are expecting in the future as well.
