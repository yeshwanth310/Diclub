Inception

About the Event



Inception was the innovatory contest conducted by the Design and Innovation club as the inaugural event. The team maximum of 2 participants was asked to submit their innovative ideas on 1st March 2019 online on which they wanted to bring it to this life and the top teams were selected to the finals where they gave a brief presentation about their idea on 4th March 2019. The contest was open to all departments and the winners got exciting prizes.


Event: Inception
Date of event : 1st and 4th March 2019
